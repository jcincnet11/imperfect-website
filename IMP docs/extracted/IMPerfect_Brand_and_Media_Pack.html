04 — Brand Rules
Do's & Don'ts
Do
  • Use charcoal (#3A3A3A) as the primary background
  • Use lime green (#C8E400) for accents and CTAs only
  • Use Barlow Condensed Black for all headers
  • Keep the "IM" prefix visually distinct (green or bold)
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M"
  • Use white text on dark backgrounds for readability
Don't
  • Don't place the logo on busy or colorful backgrounds
  • Don't stretch or distort any brand assets
  • Don't use green as a background fill — it's an accent only
  • Don't use fonts other than Barlow Condensed / Barlow
  • Don't change the "IM" prefix color in the wordmark
  • Don't use low-opacity overlays that reduce readability
05 — Voice & Tone
Brand Personality
Competitive
We play to win. Our language is driven, focused, and goal-oriented. We celebrate wins hard and learn from losses.
Authentic
No fake hype. We speak directly to our community with honesty, personality, and respect. Real people, real gaming.
Bold
Big statements. Clear visuals. Strong opinions. IMPerfect Org doesn't blend in — we stand out and own our identity.
Writing Examples
Do
"GG to our opponents. We're coming back stronger next week. 💪 #IMPerfect"
Don't
Formal corporate speak or generic phrases like "We are pleased to announce…"
Do
"Drop your IGN below — we're holding open tryouts for our Marvel Rivals roster. 🦸"
Don't
Overly aggressive trash talk or content that disrespects other orgs or players publicly.
