## Team Hub

There's a private section at **/team-hub** for players and coaches only.

To get in, you log in with your Discord account. Your Discord ID needs to be on the approved list — if you can't get in, reach out and we'll add you.

Inside you'll find:
- **Dashboard** — Weekly session overview and your next scheduled block
- **Schedule** — Full weekly practice/scrim/tournament schedule per team (IMPerfect, Shadows, Echoes)
- **Availability** — Mark which days you're available each week so coaches can plan around you
- **Manage Players** — Coaches and admins only, for managing the roster
